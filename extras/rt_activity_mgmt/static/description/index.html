Activity Management | Activity Dashboard | Activity Monitoring | Activity Views

Activity management is used to administer the activities assigned to employees in an organization. Prioritize your day and work the way you work then track them through views like list, kanban, calendar, pivot, graph and dashboards. Activity management provides an efficient way to manage and assign activities across the team to ensure harmony.
