INVOICE MULTIPLE PAYMENT

This module will help to make payments for multiple invoices, vendor bills and credit note credit note at one shot. In the payment screen automatically the open invoices gets loaded for the partner selected. Vendor bills and customer invoices can be made payment fully or partially.

Features

  • Multiple Payments in one click.

  • Full and Partial Invoice Payments.

  • Automatic Balancing of Amounts.

  • Payment mount can be written off and mark the invoice as fully paid.

HOW TO USE THIS MODULE

Here there are two open invoices for customer B.

When related to Invoice in Customer/ Payments , an payment form screen is visible, where the Payment type is by default Receive Money and the Partner Type is customer. Open invoices appears in the invoice lines automatically or also by clicking on the update invoice button, updated open invoices can be seen. .

In Payment amount field, either partial payment or full payment amount that needs to be paid need to be specified. It will automatically split into allocation amount for two invoices.Allocation amount can also be modified manually according to your choice.

After confirmation, the status will be posted, and the reconciled entries are visible.

Since, we did not make full payment, one of the invoice status is open and other is paid.
Same like customer invoices, vendor bills also can be managed very efficiently.

Now, there are two open bills for the Vendor B.

Since, it is related to vendor bills, Payment screen can be viewed under Vendors/Payments menu. The Payment type by default is Send Money and the partner type is Vendor. Same like as we have seen in the invoice, payment amount can be specified. In some cases, it is possible that you dont want to pay the full amount and want to write off the remaining amount and mark it as paid. In such cases, you need to mention the actual total payable amount in the payment amount field. In the allocation amount, you can specify the amount you will be paying and write off the remaining amount Here, as seen in the above image, The total amount is 1260, and among the first invoice the value is 525 and the allocation amount is 500. So the difference of 25 should be written off.

Here, you can see the Variance amount captures the difference of 25. And also a variance account to be specified, so that the variance amount can be passed into the specified account.

After confirming, you can see the bills are now in fully paid state.
